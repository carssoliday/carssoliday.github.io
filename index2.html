Welcome to My Website Page

By: Carson Soliday

04/01/2022

Good morning. My name is Carson Soliday and today I am going to be explaining to you some of my favorite things. I believe all of these things make me who I am. I am a unique individual with likes and dislikes and without this project, you would probably know none of these things. I will be discussing my favorite shows, foods, colors, animals, and most importantly, my music.

To begin, I will talk to you about my favorite shows. Netflix has always been a safe place for me and I can say without a doubt that the best show ever created is a tie between New Girl and the Office. New girl is a quality show about a girl who moves in with 4 male roommates. The Office follows several employees working at a paper company. Both are funny and heartfelt, which is what I love. I love watching these shows with my dad and brother.

Next, I will discuss my favorite color and animal. My favorite color is green. I was born with magnificent green eyes that border on an aquamarine color. They are beautiful in my opinion and one of my best features. I love green shoes, water bottles, green accessories, and more. My favorite animal is a bit odd, but I love hippos. They are so big and loveable and even though they are violent they are still my favorite animal.

To conclude, I will talk about my music taste. I would say my music taste balances between Indie and Rock. I love a good fem band but also adore acoustic guitar and sensitive music. Some bands and artists I love are Cage the Elephant, Tame Impala, Phoebe Bridgers, Taylor Swift, and paramore. I play guitar and piano and love to sing all the time. If I didn't have music I would not be myself. Thank you for reading!
